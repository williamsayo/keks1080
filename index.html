ABOUT

I'm a Nigerian designer with an avid passion for all things inspired by creativity. However I am only involved in graphic design related projects. My forte is logo design and brand identity as well as posters.

I am currently an architecture student of the University of Lagos and at present i'm an established designer working from my home based studio.

As a designer, I strive to offer what the client looks to gain, a functional and professional design solution.
I often like to communicate with the client as much as possible as the project advances so we can come out with the best possible design solution. My portfolio, in it's own way, is really quite diverse in range containning everything from logo design to posters, even artworks which I made to improve my portfolio. The phrase "love what you do and do what you love" springs to mind. I would prefer to let my work speak for itself so feel free to flick through.
